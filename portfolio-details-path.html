Path generator

Path generator is an innovative application that leverages the power of Flutter for its frontend and Django for its backend, aimed at improving the fitness levels of elderly people. The app takes expert-defined paths and transforms them into easy-to-follow steps, making exercise a more manageable and accessible experience for seniors. With DidiE, elderly individuals can now stay physically active, promoting their overall health and well-being.
